Revolutionising ‘learning’ by delivering practical knowledge for changemakers & helping them reach their full potential.( August ‘21 ~ February ‘22 )

SkillsUnion is a digital learning platform working with leading universities and renowned employers to design & deliver digital skill training programmes that help our students succeed in today’s fast-evolving global tech industry.
SkillsUnion acts as a partnership between learners, universities, and employers, delivering online, part-time & vocational courses that accelerate careers & businesses. Their sole mission is to unlock the true potential of their students by equipping them with the skills, tools and mindset to help them succeed in today’s rapidly evolving digital economy.
‍
In contrast to other traditional ed-tech platforms, SkillsUnion pairs their learners/students with a personal career coach, guiding them through a proven career acceleration framework designed to help them match employment opportunities upon completing their courses.
Project Overview
As the team’s only designer, I had to juggle quite a few tasks and roles simultaneously. One of which was to work on their new brand identity — SkillsUnion helps people from all backgrounds and offers diversity & inclusion scholarships in all their programs while actively working with their education partners to help create a fairer, more equitable world for all. And this needed to be showcased through our new branding.

So we worked on a new colour identity that reflected the same energy, determination, and spirit of learning while filling up the forward-looking sense our previous palette lacked. The newly redesigned SkillsUnion supported our aim and morals by using a broad-spectrum inclusive colour palette, playful shapes/illustrations, constructive type, and bold human photography.
My Role
Product Design
(Growth / Marketing)
Year
2021 - 22
Besides redefining the new SkillsUnion brand and its core identity, I also helped establish an integrated design system for their then recently expanded web products ; And a dedicated style guide to act as a single source of truth for both product and future marketing design initiatives.
Creating a spacing system was an essential part of our design system. The spacing between the design components / elements were incrementally measured to create a harmonious arrangement. This helped create a predictable rhythm within our designs, thus giving the overall experience a sense of intentionality and well-designed architecture.

Spacing goes beyond mathematical precision as it also reacts to the objects it surrounds by giving more space to larger objects and less to the smaller ones. 'Optical adjustments' were also introduced to the system, to be used when an element looks off or the spacing needs a nudge to make things visually feel right.
