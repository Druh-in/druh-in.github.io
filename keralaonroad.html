WHAT IS KERALAONROAD ?
KeralaOnRoad is an online consumer-focused platform that connects car buyers and sellers in Kerala. It aims to provide a one-stop solution for consumers in Kerala making car-purchase decisions easier than ever.
Team & Timeline
I joined the team as a UX/UI intern to help with the final product design of their web platform. I worked directly with the Product Manager and collaborated with the backend team on the deployment of new features that aimed to help customers make the right decisions while buying a car. My tasks in the first half of my internship involved designing and optimizing the old UI. And the later half consisted of me working on creating coded prototypes of the finalized UI.

By working together with the dev team, all of my designs were implemented in a few weeks. As a result, in just one month, we saw a 35% increase in overall user engagement along with a significant increase in conversions too.
